S3CSS

Simplest Stylesheet You'll Ever Find

Welcome and thank you for using S3CSS (pronounced 'streaks'), a lightweight CSS/JS open source framework (coded in SCSS and jQuery) to help you create simple, responsive website templates. It was developed by J. Djimitry Riviere in late 2016, and continues to be improved upon by its developer. The major key to this framework lies within its ease of us to deliver superb results when creating a website. Not only that, it is and will always remain FREE to use; though in exchange, providing feedback and help find ways to improve the framework further would be appreciated.


You can replace this paragraph at any time, when you are developing your page with the wonderful S3CSS Framework.
