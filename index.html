SPARKLE

Welcome and thank you for using Sparkle, a lightweight CSS open source framework to help you create simple, responsive website templates. It was developed by J. Djimitry Riviere in late 2016, and continues to be improved upon by its developer. The major key to this framework lies within its ease of us to deliver superb results when designing apps and websites. Not only that, it is and will always remain FREE to use; though in exchange, providing feedback and help find ways to improve the framework further would be appreciated.

Sparkle is...

Semantics

Sparkle uses BEM to keep the framework structured and simple for all to understand and use.

User-Friendly

Sparkle does most of the hard work for you. Simply style your elements.

Responsive

Sparkle was created to be responsive throughout all devices, using a mobile-first approach.

Lightweight

Sparkle is smaller than 20kb, which means it won't slow down your website loading.
